Membership Levels

Non-Profit

Cost: Free

Access to networking events: Attend chamber-hosted networking events to connect with other businesses and community leaders.

Educational resources: Gain access to educational workshops, seminars, and webinars on topics relevant to non-profit organizations.

Listing in the chamber's directory: Get listed in the chamber's online and printed directory to increase visibility within the business community.

Bronze

Cost: $50/year

Enhanced directory listing: Stand out with an enhanced listing in the chamber's directory, including a detailed profile and prominent placement.

Event discounts: Enjoy discounted rates or exclusive access to chamber-sponsored events, conferences, workshops, and seminars.

Silver

Cost: $100/year

Advertising opportunities: Showcase your organization through advertising options such as banner ads or sponsored content on the chamber's website or newsletter.

Recognition and awards: Get opportunities for recognition through awards, certificates, or mentions in the chamber's publications or at special events.

Gold

Cost: $150/year

VIP treatment: Receive personalized support from the chamber's staff, including dedicated account management and priority assistance.

Spotlight positions: Enjoy prominent positioning on the chamber's website, homepage, or event materials, maximizing your organization's exposure.

Sponsorship opportunities: Gain priority consideration for sponsorship opportunities at chamber events, providing additional brand visibility.
